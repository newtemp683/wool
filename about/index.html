About Woolworths Market

Our Commitment to Quality and Value

Founded by Percy Christmas, Woolworths transformed grocery shopping with a focus on quality and customer satisfaction, setting a high standard in the retail industry.

With nearly a century of experience, Woolworths Market has built a reputation as Australia’s trusted grocery retailer. Our extensive knowledge of sourcing fresh produce and quality meats ensures that every customer finds the best for their families. We pride ourselves on our dedicated team and community engagement.

Meet the Founder

Woolworths was founded by Frank Woolworth, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for retail. His commitment to quality and customer satisfaction laid the foundation for what Woolworths Market represents today.

Frank Woolworth started his journey in 1924, inspired by a desire to provide affordable groceries to every household. His mission to create a community-focused shopping experience continues to drive our values and operations.
